# Danny's Personal Website — Foundation Document

## Vision

A personal website that holds the full dimensionality of who Danny is — not a portfolio, not a blog, but a living architecture for a person who moves between engineering leadership, poetry, philosophy, music, and the deep relational work of becoming.

---

## Architecture: The House Metaphor

The site is organized as rooms in a house. Each room has a distinct atmosphere and purpose, but they share walls. A visitor can wander.

### Rooms

| Room | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **The Foyer** | Entry point. Who Danny is, how to orient. The first impression and the invitation inward. |
| **The Studio** | Professional and technical work. Engineering, leadership, craft-as-devotion. Professionally legible but not corporate. |
| **The Garden** | Poetry. Living, growing, seasonal. Work that breathes. |
| **The Study** | Personal essays, philosophy, deeper thinking. The quiet room with good light. |
| **The Salon** | Music, aesthetics, art. Where beauty circulates between people. The cellist's son's room. |

---

## Content Model

Each piece of content on the site is called a **work**.

A work can live in one room but be tagged with multiple **facets** — the dimensions of Danny's life and thought that cut across rooms.

---

## Facets

Facets are the cross-cutting dimensions. A work in The Studio might be tagged with `craft` and `leadership`. A poem in The Garden might carry `language` and `body`. Facets allow visitors to trace threads across rooms.

| Facet | What it holds |
|---|---|
| **craft** | How things are made. The care in the making. Technique, tools, the hand and the material. |
| **consciousness** | Awareness, interiority, presence. The inner life examined. |
| **language** | Words as medium, as music, as meaning-making. The love of what language can do. |
| **leadership** | Building containers for others. Management philosophy, organizational thinking, the craft of leading. |
| **beauty** | The aesthetic dimension. What moves us and why. Not decoration — encounter. |
| **becoming** | The autotelic unfolding of personhood over time. Not awareness (that's consciousness), not thinking about it — the actual forward motion of a person revealing themselves to themselves. |
| **relation** | The space between. Authentic Relating, management-as-container, meaning-philosophy. The interpersonal ground where two can become. |
| **body** | The gymnast, the stutterer who found theater, muscle memory before thought. Consciousness without body floats — this is the ground. |

### Parked: Future Modes

These are not facets (topics/dimensions) but potential **modes** (lenses/registers) that could layer over the system later:

- **devotion** — The quality of sustained attention. What the cellist parents modeled. The register, not the output. Craft can be devotional, but devotion can also be stillness.
- **play** — The gearless mode. Integration through non-orchestration. The anti-calcification agent that keeps the system from becoming another thing to live up to.

These are architecturally distinct from facets — they describe *how* rather than *what*. We're holding space for them without embedding them yet.

---

## Design Principles

- **Dimensionality over simplicity.** Danny is not one thing. The site should hold complexity without becoming complicated.
- **Warmth over polish.** Professional legibility, yes. Corporate sheen, no.
- **Wandering over wayfinding.** The architecture invites exploration, not just navigation.
- **Living over finished.** The site grows. Works accumulate. The garden is tended, not completed.
